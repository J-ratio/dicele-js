Dicele is a unique and challenging daily number puzzle game that has quickly gained popularity among puzzle enthusiasts. The objective of the game is to solve the puzzle in 21 moves by swapping the dice on the board to match the sum mentioned on the row and column.

One of the standout features of Dicele is its share feature, which allows players to share their stats on social media platforms such as Twitter and Whatsapp. This feature adds an extra layer of competition and fun to the game, as players can compare their scores and compete with friends to see who can solve the puzzle in the fewest moves.
